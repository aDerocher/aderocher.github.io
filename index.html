Hurton Snowboarding

For my capstone project at AppAcademy, I created a clone of the online snowboard shop Burton.com.

  • Shopping for items (with or without an account)
  • Leaving/Editing reviews on purchased items
  • Keeping items on wishlists
  • Editing account details
